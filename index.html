But where did Parkoo come from??

As my kids have grown older and our time together becomes more and more important, the realization that I don't have hours to search the web coupled with my boys' desire to go to new places culminated to form our own park finder. A list of places we'd visited with the features offered at each location.
Thanks to technology, we now want to offer our "research" to everyone to get out and find something new and beautiful to share with their loved ones, their pets or even their own inner child.
While this application is in no way perfect, I hope it at least helps you and those who matter most to you find somewhere new to play, fish, or just get outside.
